It's always been about the people.

When Bordertown opened in 2004, it ran on inspiration and passion through all the inexperience and lessons learned when starting a business.


But the unique location, and more importantly, the people who visited and the people who served them kept this place alive. Both in customers and staff, Bordertown Coffee has been blessed by the thousands upon thousands who have experienced it.

Our Values

At Bordertown we want people to feel seen. It's more than about coffee and cash changing hands, we want to get to know you and what you're passionate about.


Here at Bordertown, you can expect us to act in accordance with our five core values: Build Relationships, Craft Beverages, Impeccable Cleanliness, Cool Headed Under Pressure, and Unlock Doors.
